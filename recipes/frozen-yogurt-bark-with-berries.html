Frozen Yogurt Bark with Berries

Description

Reach for these instead of candy to appease your kid's sweet tooth while keeping them healthy. Win win!

Stay cool & healthy with the easy to make & tasty treats for the summertime. Really anytime you're craving berries.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams vanilla frozen yogurt
  • 1 tbsp. maple syrup
  • 200 grams berries
  • 20 grams pistachios

Steps

  1. Line a shallow baking tray with a parchment paper.
  2. In a high-speed blender, add the yogurt & maple syrup and blend until smooth. Pour it into the prepared baking tray and spread it smooth with the back of a spoon.
  3. Sprinkle the desired toppings - Berries & Nuts.
  4. Freeze the yogurt mixture for 2-3 hours or overnight until set. Cut into squares and serve cold, or store in a container in the freezer.
